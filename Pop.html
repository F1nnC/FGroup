What is Pop Music?

Pop music originates in the United States and the United Kingdom around the 1950s. Pop music has good rhythm and a catchy melody.

Top Five Pop Artist

Bruno Mars

Bruno Mars is one of the most popular pop artist and is compared to Michael Jackson, his music is very expansive. His early music focused on heartbreak whilst his music now focuses on happy club-ish music.

Katy Perry

Katy Perry is very unique pop artist and has won many awards for her songs. She mostly sings about past relationships and is compared to Lady Gaga. She performed in Super Bowl XLIX and it was great. Overall, she is a very popular music artist.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is one of if not the best selling artist of all time. She has a large collection of top charting albums and singles. She changed the whole entire music industry. Some would argue she IS the music industry!

Rihanna

Rihanna is a very popular pop artist. She was inspired by Janet Jackson, Madonna, and Bob Marley. She is compared to Beyonce. Some would argue she is an R and B artist but she has lots of pop qualities. Her music varies quite a bit overall.

Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande rose to fame in her acting days as she joined the show "Sam and Cat" and "Victorious" she contiuned her success througn her melodical notes. She is very tlented and can hit very high notes. Her music is self impowering and woman impowering. She sings about loads of things.
